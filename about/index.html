About Us

Florida Lawyers in Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton and Palm Beach

Founded in 1990, Young, Berman, Karpf & Karpf, P.A. combines experience, scholarship and talent in focused areas of legal practice. The firm’s lawyers routinely join efforts, drawing on expertise from their respective practice areas to collaborate on complex cases and use all available resources to fulfill clients’ objectives. As part of our goal to provide excellent legal services, the firm stays abreast of technological advances and employs the most advanced case-management technology for efficient handling of legal matters. Our attorneys take a personalized approach to each case, maintaining direct and close contact with our clients. Many of the firm’s cases originate through referrals from other law firms in Florida and the United States.

Recognitions – Honesty, Integrity & Professionalism

Having acquired a well-earned national reputation, our achievements are distinguished by the fact that we strive to provide each and every client with the superior legal representation they deserve. Each of our partners is hard-working and exceptionally skilled and talented in their particular practice.  Mitchell Karpf, Esq. is the Past Chair of the Family Law Section of the American Bar Association.

Board Certification

All of our principals are Board Certified by the Florida Bar Board of Legal Specialization and Education. Attorneys Mitchell K. Karpf, Brian Karpf, Andrew Wilson are board certified in Marital and Family Law, and Attorney Andrew S. Berman is board certified in Appellate Practice.

AV Rated by Lexis Nexis, Martindale-Hubbell,

Also an AV rated firm, our attorneys are listed in the Martindale-Hubbell Bar Register of Preeminent Lawyers.

“The Martindale-Hubbell Bar Register is limited to only the most distinguished law practices; those that have achieved the AV rating in the Martindale-Hubbell Law Directory. The “A” signifies the highest level of legal ability, while the “V” denotes “very high” adherence to the professional standards of conduct, ethics, reliability and diligence.”
